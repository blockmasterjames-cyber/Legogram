Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

BrickFeed ("we", "our", or "us") operates the BrickFeed mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use BrickFeed, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and display name that you provide during registration.
  • Profile Information: Optional bio text and profile/background photos you upload.
  • User Content: Photos, videos, descriptions, and comments you post to BrickFeed.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you use the App (pages visited, features used) collected through Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your BrickFeed account.
  • To display your posts, comments, and profile to other users.
  • To operate the social features of the App (likes, comments, follows, direct messages).
  • To enforce our community guidelines and keep BrickFeed safe.
  • To improve the App experience through aggregated, anonymized analytics.

3. Firebase and Third-Party Services

BrickFeed uses Google Firebase for:

  • Firebase Authentication: To securely manage user sign-in (email/password and Sign in with Apple).
  • Cloud Firestore: To store user profiles, posts, and comments.
  • Firebase Storage: To store photos and videos you upload.
  • Firebase Analytics: To collect anonymized usage statistics. No personally identifiable information is shared with advertisers.

Firebase services are governed by the Google Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

BrickFeed is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

  • We do not knowingly collect more personal information from children than is reasonably necessary to provide the App's features.
  • We do not use children's data for targeted advertising or behavioral profiling.
  • Parents or guardians are responsible for creating accounts for children under 13. The "Kid Safe Mode" feature provides additional content restrictions.
  • A parent or guardian may contact us at any time to review, delete, or stop the collection of their child's personal information.
  • All user-generated content passes through our built-in bad word filter to maintain a safe environment.
  • Direct messaging requires age verification to protect younger users.

5. No Advertising

BrickFeed does not display any third-party advertisements. We do not share your data with advertising networks or data brokers.

6. No Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Your data is used solely to operate BrickFeed.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time through the Settings screen in the App. When you delete your account, we will remove your profile data, posts, and comments within 30 days.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time by deleting your account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the App. Continued use of BrickFeed after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
